19.jun.2006 - Tour North-Nolland

We completed a 200-km tour through "Noord-Holland". The trail followed the original ice-skating tour with a sidetrack to the Callantsoog beach. You can view the tracks in the "Show | Track Archive" menu and pictures through the "Show | Photo Albums" menu.

04.jun.2006 - New site with Google Maps

www.geoskating.com has been completely renewed. This is made possible by building on GeoTracing, a generic mapping/tracing application framework. Though improvements to the design are pending, there is much more to see and explore. Most importantly this site is totally based on Google Maps. Just try out different options in the menu. Hint: do Show|Autoplay Tracks to see tracks with media played back.
