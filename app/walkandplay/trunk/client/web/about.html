What is GeoTracing?

Geotracing is a mobile super multi-user location sharing game, which you can fully design yourself. Set out your own traces, make tours and let others participate with their mobile phones and look them up on the maps. Isn’t that great? Or what!

How to join the fun?

We just could not make it simpler. Just click on sign up and create an profile. From then on, you just have to sign in! Find your friend on the map, search your favourite tags and join other groups that have set out to create new adventures of their own. Completely free!

What media to share?

You can share almost every possible type of media: movies, photographs, text or sound. Record them with your mobile phone and send them while you are playing your game or recording your trace. When creating your game you can also add your media to your points of interest along the trace. These will be visible to all players that have joined. Every regis- tered user of GeoTracing can join groups of create routes of their own. Fantastic stuff up for grabs!

Use the maps to navigate, or click on the icons to go to archived routes of to search for friends. When you are logged in, you can go to your profile and alter any data you entered so far, including your profile information, your routes, friends and submitted data. Join now and have fun.
