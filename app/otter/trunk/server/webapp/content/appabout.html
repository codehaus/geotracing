About OtterTracing

The OtterTracing application supports field observations on otter-activity and -dispersion in the Netherlands. Through the MobiTracer Mobile GPS application locations are assembled (as POIs) on various aspects of otter activity like traces, holes, and "spraints" (otter faeces).

Security

Observation data is password-protected since this is sensitive information.
